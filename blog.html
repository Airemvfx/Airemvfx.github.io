Matt finally hits a kickflip!

Oh my goodness, hold onto your hats and prepare for a miracle because it finally happened - Matt has landed a kickflip! The world must be coming to an end because this is truly a once-in-a-lifetime occurrence. It's almost as if the planets aligned and the stars shone down upon him, guiding his feet to finally make contact with the skateboard at just the right angle. You could practically hear the angels singing as the board flipped over and Matt landed it like a pro. I mean, who would have thought that someone who can barely stand on a skateboard, let alone do any tricks, could actually land a kickflip? It's like watching a penguin suddenly take flight and soar through the sky. We might as well nominate Matt for the next Olympics because if he can pull off a kickflip, who knows what other incredible feats he's capable of. Maybe he'll be the first person to do a triple backflip while balancing a plate of spaghetti on his head. The possibilities are endless!
